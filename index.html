About Us

Our founders share a creative flair and a distaste for throwing anything away!

Jen Horn

A proud Lancastrian, Jen lives in Garstang and runs the Dallas Road After School Club in Lancaster.

Jen has always been artistic and has discovered a passion for turning unloved items of furniture into desirable contemporary objects.

Bena Mboroga-Horn

Born in Meru, Kenya, Bena moved to London in the late 90s, meeting Jen's brother Mike in 2011. They moved "back" to Lancashire in 2018.

Growing up in rural Kenya, Bena has always been an advocate of reuse and repair. She is now enjoying working with Jen to share this ethic with the good people of her adopted home town of Lancaster.
